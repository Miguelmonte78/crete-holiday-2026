Acceptance

1. Agreement to use this website

By using this website, the user agrees to use it as an informational travel planning tool only. The website is not a booking platform, emergency service, official tourism authority, medical provider, legal advisor, navigation authority or financial advisor.

Information accuracy

2. Travel information may change

Prices: museum fees, restaurant estimates, fuel costs, parking fees and activity prices may change.
Opening hours: archaeological sites, museums, restaurants, markets and events may change opening times or close unexpectedly.
Routes: driving times, road conditions, parking access and beach access can vary due to traffic, weather, construction or local restrictions.
Events: festivals, panigiria, concerts and cultural events should be verified locally before planning the day around them.
Weather and sea conditions: wind, waves, heat and visibility can change quickly and should always be checked before beach, snorkeling or walking plans.

Safety

3. Safety and decision responsibility

Emergency

In an emergency, call local emergency services immediately. Do not rely on the website for urgent help.

Call 112 in emergency

Driving

Drivers remain responsible for route choice, driving behaviour, road rules, parking decisions and rental car conditions.

Verify live route

Beaches and walks

Travellers should assess heat, wind, waves, footwear, water supply, family energy and local warnings before continuing.

Safety first

Budget and OCR

4. Budget tracker and receipt scanning

Budget estimates: daily cost estimates, restaurant estimates and activity costs are planning aids, not guaranteed prices.
Actual spending: expenses entered by the user are saved locally and should be checked for accuracy.
Receipt OCR: OCR may misread totals, dates, merchant names or currencies. The user should confirm or correct all OCR results before saving.
Financial decisions: the budget dashboard is for personal trip tracking and should not be treated as financial advice.

External services

5. Third-party links and services

The website may link to external services such as Google Maps, Tripadvisor, restaurant websites, map tiles, Bootstrap, Google Fonts, Chart.js, OCR libraries or official tourism/event websites. These external services are outside the control of this website and may have separate terms, privacy policies, cookies, fees, availability limitations or data use practices.

Opening external links is optional and should be done with normal online safety judgment.

Personal data

6. Personal information and local storage

Local storage: visited places, packing checklist progress, meal costs and expense entries may be stored in the browser.
Personal contact details: if the user adds accommodation, rental car, insurance or family contact details, the user is responsible for deciding where and how those details are stored.
Public publishing: before publishing the site on GitHub Pages or another public host, remove personal addresses, phone numbers, booking references, insurance references and private travel details.
Privacy page: review the Privacy page for more practical information about local browser storage and deletion.

Copyright and images

7. Content, photos and image use

The website may use representative images, local photos, placeholders or links to external photo pages. Only publish photos that the family owns, has permission to use, or that are legally reusable under the relevant licence.

Do not copy restaurant, Tripadvisor, Google, tourism-board or photographer images into the website unless permission/licence allows it.

Limitations

8. Limitation of responsibility

The website is provided as-is for personal planning. The user is responsible for checking information, making safe choices, following local laws, respecting local guidance, managing spending, and deciding whether an activity, route, beach, restaurant or event is suitable for the family.
